### **📌 ALS Care Team: Medical & Health Professionals Involved Throughout the Disease Stages**
🚀 **ALS patients require a multidisciplinary team at different stages of progression.**  
This list covers **core specialists**, plus **additional referrals for atypical cases.**  

---

## **📖 ALS Care Team by Stage of Disease Progression**
### **🟢 Early Stage (Diagnostic Phase & Symptom Onset)**
1️⃣ **Primary Care Physician (PCP) 🏥** – The first doctor ALS patients typically visit.  
   - **Refers to a neurologist after ruling out common conditions.**

2️⃣ **Neurologist 🧠** (ALS Specialist) – Central figure in ALS diagnosis & management.  
   - **Performs EMG, clinical assessment, confirms ALS diagnosis.**  
   - **Refers to additional specialists for symptom management.**  
   - Subspecialists: **Motor Neuron Disease (MND) Specialists, ALS Research Neurologists.**

3️⃣ **Genetic Counselor 🧬** (For familial ALS cases)  
   - **Discusses genetic risks & testing for family members.**  
   - **Helps with clinical trial eligibility for gene-targeted therapies.**

4️⃣ **Social Worker 🏡** (Medical or ALS Clinic Social Worker)  
   - **Coordinates support resources, financial planning, and disability benefits.**  
   - **Assists with home care planning & insurance approvals.**  

---

### **🟡 Mid-Stage ALS (Functional Decline & Adaptive Care)**
5️⃣ **Physical Therapist (PT) 🦵** – Focus on mobility & muscle preservation.  
   - **Exercises to maintain strength & prevent contractures.**  
   - **Recommends adaptive equipment (e.g., braces, walkers, wheelchairs).**  

6️⃣ **Occupational Therapist (OT) ✋** – Focus on daily activities & assistive devices.  
   - **Helps patients use adaptive tools for eating, dressing, and mobility.**  
   - **Recommends home modifications (e.g., ramps, grab bars).**  

7️⃣ **Speech-Language Pathologist (SLP) 🗣️** – Helps with speech & swallowing.  
   - **Early-stage: Speech therapy to slow decline.**  
   - **Mid-stage: Introduces AAC (Augmentative & Alternative Communication) devices.**  
   - **Later stage: Manages swallowing issues to prevent choking.**  

8️⃣ **Dietitian / Nutritionist 🥗** – Prevents weight loss & malnutrition.  
   - **Creates high-calorie meal plans to combat hypermetabolism.**  
   - **Advises on thickened liquids & pureed diets as swallowing declines.**  
   - **Recommends PEG (feeding tube) when necessary.**  

9️⃣ **Respiratory Therapist (RT) 🌬️** – Manages breathing difficulties.  
   - **Monitors lung function (Forced Vital Capacity - FVC tests).**  
   - **Provides Non-Invasive Ventilation (NIV/BiPAP) support.**  

🔟 **Palliative Care Specialist 🏥** – Provides symptom relief & comfort-focused care.  
   - **Helps with pain, breathlessness, anxiety, and quality of life.**  
   - **Can be involved at any stage, not just end-of-life.**  

---

### **🔴 Late-Stage ALS (Ventilatory & End-of-Life Care)**
1️⃣1️⃣ **Pulmonologist (Lung Specialist) 🫁** – Manages ventilatory support needs.  
   - **Evaluates when to transition from NIV (BiPAP) to invasive ventilation (tracheostomy).**  

1️⃣2️⃣ **Hospice & End-of-Life Care Team ⚕️** – Provides comfort care in advanced ALS.  
   - **Manages pain, anxiety, and respiratory distress.**  
   - **Coordinates at-home or hospice facility care.**  

---

## **📖 Additional Specialists for Atypical ALS Presentations**
### **🟠 If ALS Presents with Cognitive Impairment (ALS-FTD Spectrum):**
1️⃣ **Neuropsychologist 🧠** – Evaluates cognitive decline & behavior changes.  
2️⃣ **Psychiatrist/Psychologist 🧑‍⚕️** – Manages depression, anxiety, & FTD-related aggression.  

### **🟠 If ALS Mimics an Autoimmune or Inflammatory Disease:**
3️⃣ **Rheumatologist 🏥** – Rules out inflammatory conditions like **MMN, Lupus, Myositis.**  
4️⃣ **Immunologist 🦠** – Evaluates immune system involvement if ALS-like symptoms are unclear.  

### **🟠 If ALS Has Rapid Progression or Severe Early Bulbar Symptoms:**
5️⃣ **Gastroenterologist (GI Specialist) 🍽️** – **Oversees feeding tube placement** (PEG tube).  
6️⃣ **ENT (Otolaryngologist) 🏥** – **Manages swallowing & vocal cord dysfunction.**  

### **🟠 If the Patient Considers Experimental or Investigational Therapies:**
7️⃣ **Clinical Trial Coordinator 🔬** – Helps **enroll patients in ALS drug trials.**  
8️⃣ **Stem Cell Therapy Specialist 🧬** – If **investigating cell-based ALS treatments.**
